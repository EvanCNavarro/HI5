About Us

Who is LHI?

Letelier Home Improvements is a family-run company, founded in 1987. We specialize in both Property Management and Home Improvement; primarily working within the Port St. Lucie and Fort Pierce area.

For over 20 years we've been dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and affordable living to our tenants at the Mayflower Apartments.

• The purpose of this site is to provide general information for any new applicants that are interested in renting with us. Reach out to us if you have any questions, via the "Contact Us" page.
